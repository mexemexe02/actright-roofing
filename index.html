What We Do

Complete Roofing Services

From emergency leak repairs to full-scale replacements, our skilled team handles every aspect of residential and commercial roofing with precision and care.

Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and replacement using premium asphalt shingles, metal roofing, or flat roof systems. Complete cleanup included.

Leak Detection & Repair

Pinpoint the source of roof leaks fast. We fix damaged flashing, worn seals, cracked vents, and missing shingles before water damage spreads.

Flat Roof Systems

EPDM, TPO, and modified bitumen flat roofing for commercial buildings, garages, and modern residential designs. Built to last.

Ice & Water Shield

Proper ice dam prevention with premium underlayment. Critical for Ontario winters — we install it right the first time.

Soffit, Fascia & Eavestrough

Complete exterior trim replacement and gutter systems. Seamless aluminum eavestroughs, custom fascia, and vented soffit for proper airflow.

Roof Inspections

Buying or selling a home? Get a thorough roof inspection report. We assess condition, remaining lifespan, and document any issues.
